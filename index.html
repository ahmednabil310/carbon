How To Buy

to adopt some bingus of your own, currently you will need to use pancakeswap. bingus on pancakeswap is tradeable using either metamask on chrome or trustwallet on your mobile device.

if you have any issues, head over to our friendly telegram community where we will be happy to help!

1.

Download MetaMask or use an existing wallet

Head To Metamask.Io And Download Their Wallet To Your Phone Or Chrome/Firefox Browser

2.

Go to pancakeswap.finance and‍ make sure it's set to "v1 (old)"

PancakeSwap is where you'll be performing the swap of your current tokens to CARBON.

3.

Get to the trade screen‍

Click "Connect" at the top right of the screen, and then navigate to "Trade" on the left sidebar.

4.

Select the CARBON token‍

Click on the "Select a Currency" button, and enter the CARBON token contract: 0x5e90253fbae4dab78aa351f4e6fed08a64ab5590

5.

Adjust your slippage to 12%‍

Click "Settings" at the top right, and adjust your slippage to 12% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is).

6.

Swap away!‍

Enter the amount you want to buy and swap away! Remember to have enough BNB for gas fees!
